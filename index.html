What does Rotaract do?

Rotaract originally began as a Rotary International youth program in 1968. "Rotaract" stands for "Rotary in Action". It is a service, leadership, professional and community service organization (often miscommunicated as a Social Service Club) for young men and women between the ages 16-31. Rotaract is an international youth organization that undertakes the process of creating responsible citizens of the world to bring about the positive change. Our Year Theme

Connecting the Dots

It is really important for any organization to have unity within it. The vision of the club this year is to achieve all the goals of Rotaract but as a single unit working together. Connecting the dots has its meaning in keeping all the dots of the club connected. The club strongly believes in the saying, "Together We Rise, Divided we fall. " Keeping it mind, the year theme, "Connecting the Dots" strongly portrays the prime focus of the club. The Rotaract Club of Northern Suburbs together will Connect the Dots to Make it Happen!
